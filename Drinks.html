Popular Coffee Drinks
Drinks Preparation Temperature Ingredients Details
Cappuccino Espresso Hot Equal amount of espresso and steam milk Pour the espresso through the milk and serve
Latte Espresso Hot 2/3 or more steamed milk and espresso Pour the espresso through the milk and serve
Macchiato Espresso Hot Espresso with just a "mark" of steamed milk Add milk to the espresso after pressing
Iced Coffee Drip or French press Cold Coffee poured over ice Can be sweetened with sugar or creme
Frappe Strong instant coffee Cold Instant coffee,water and milk Shake to create a form,then pour over ice and serve. A Specialty in Greece
Flatwhite Espresso Hot 1/3 Espresso and 2/3 Steamed milk A Specialty in Australia and New Zealand. The foam is often decorated with a Motif
